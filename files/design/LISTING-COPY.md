## Detailed description
Claude Usage Widget Lite keeps an eye on your Claude usage so you don't have to. It shows how much of your current session and weekly limits you've used, plus a live countdown to when they reset — all inside a tiny picture-in-picture window that floats on top of whatever you're working on.

Why you'll like it
› Always visible. Pops out as a picture-in-picture window that stays on top of any tab or app.
› Session + weekly at a glance. Two simple meters — no digging through menus.
› No surprises. A live "resets in" countdown tells you exactly when you're back to full.
› Featherweight. Lite by design — fast, tiny, and out of your way.
› Private & free. Runs entirely in your browser. No sign-up, no tracking, no cost.

Pin it, glance at it, and stop worrying about hitting your limit mid-thought.
