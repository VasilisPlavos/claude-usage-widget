## Detailed description
Claude Usage Widget Lite keeps an eye on your Claude usage so you don't have to. It shows how much of your current session and weekly limits you've used, plus a live countdown to when they reset and an at-a-glance check of Claude's service status — all inside a tiny picture-in-picture window that floats on top of whatever you're working on.

Why you'll like it
› Always visible. Pops out as a picture-in-picture window that stays on top of any tab or app.
› Session + weekly at a glance. Two simple meters — no digging through menus.
› No surprises. A live "resets in" countdown tells you exactly when you're back to full.
› Is Claude having issues? A status dot turns yellow or red during an incident — focus it to read Anthropic's own status message, right in the widget.
› Keep working when limits hit. If your weekly "All models" is maxed but Sonnet still has room, the widget flags Sonnet as still available — so you can switch and carry on.
› Featherweight. Lite by design — fast, tiny, and out of your way.
› Private & free. Runs entirely in your browser. No sign-up, no tracking, no cost.

Pin it, glance at it, and stop worrying about hitting your limit — or missing an outage — mid-thought.
